Biography

In this page I will talk a little bit about myself as well as my life. I am a 25 year old male who is a husband & became a father to a beautiful baby boy on February 13th 2022. I have always been interested in technology since the very beginning of my life as I was fascinated with how it works and how we are all dependent on it on a daily basis. Altough I began programming on the side such us on Codecademy and on Youtube, I quickly realized that I wanted a more structured program to learn more material and become ready for my first job in technology. This is why I enrolled in FlatIron School and I'm excited to begin in the March co-hort!

During my early years as a child I had grown up in private schools where I was always fascinated by technology and knew that this is what I wanted to pursue when I grew older. As the years went on and I eventually graduated from highschool, I enrolled in a computer science program at the local county college. I found it initially very difficult because we were consistently taught theory instead of actually coding, which deterred me away from programming and I started to switch to a more financial / analytical major. I also at this time found my first professional career as a universal banker at the age of 20 at a large financial firm.

As the time went on I was working a lot and bounced around ideas on what I wanted to do in my life as I did not have any clear cut direction and still wanted to grow professionally of course. I was taking part time college classes while working 40 hours a week which did not give me much leg room to do anything else. Regardless I decided to try to study for the finra licences because the next logical step in my career was to be an investment banker. As I studied and tried to put my head down and focus I realized I wanted something more and did not want to pursue this as my next career. I was still puzzled on what I wanted to do but, continued to grow in my current role and become a top seller in my market. I further took my skill-sets and joined the largest financial firm in the US as a Relationship Manager.
